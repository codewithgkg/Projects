About Us

This Event Planning Information system aims to provide customers with a convenient platform to customize their event experience access daily canteen menus and place orders .
Our objectives includes optimizing the event planning workflow, streamlining the ordering process, improving customer satisfaction and much more. The proposed solution incorporates features such as menu customization, event decoration material selection, and backend administration for data management.
